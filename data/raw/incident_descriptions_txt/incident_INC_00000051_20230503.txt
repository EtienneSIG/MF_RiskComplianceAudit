Incident ID: INC_00000051
Date: 2023-05-03 00:00:00
Severity: low
Type: data_breach

DESCRIPTION:
On 2023-05-03 00:00:00, a data breach was detected in the Sales department. Unauthorized access was gained to customer databases containing personally identifiable information (PII). The breach was discovered during routine security monitoring when anomalous access patterns were detected. Initial investigation suggests the breach occurred due to compromised employee credentials. Approximately 7815 customer records may have been exposed.


IMPACT ASSESSMENT:
- Financial Impact: $118,214 USD
- Severity Level: LOW
- Affected Department: Sales
- Potential Regulatory Implications: PCI-DSS non-compliance penalty



ROOT CAUSE:
Insufficient access controls and authentication mechanisms

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS TAKEN:
- Incident response team activated
- Affected systems isolated
- Senior management notified
- Forensic investigation initiated
- Legal and compliance teams engaged
