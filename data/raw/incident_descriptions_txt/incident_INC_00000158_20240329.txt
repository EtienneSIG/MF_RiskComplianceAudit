Incident ID: INC_00000158
Date: 2024-03-29 00:00:00
Severity: medium
Type: data_breach

DESCRIPTION:
On 2024-03-29 00:00:00, a data breach was detected in the Customer Support department. Unauthorized access was gained to customer databases containing personally identifiable information (PII). The breach was discovered during routine security monitoring when anomalous access patterns were detected. Initial investigation suggests the breach occurred due to compromised employee credentials. Approximately 3048 customer records may have been exposed.


IMPACT ASSESSMENT:
- Financial Impact: $115,434 USD
- Severity Level: MEDIUM
- Affected Department: Customer Support
- Potential Regulatory Implications: GDPR Article 33 breach notification required



ROOT CAUSE:
Missing or ineffective monitoring and alerting

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS TAKEN:
- Incident response team activated
- Affected systems isolated
- Senior management notified
- Forensic investigation initiated
- Legal and compliance teams engaged
